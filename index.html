About Krishi Rakshak

Krishi Rakshak is an AI-driven platform designed to assist farmers in early identification of plant diseases and crop health issues .

By leveraging computer vision and machine learning and deep learning technologies, the system analyzes crop images and provides instant disease detection support.

The objective is to promote smart farming, reduce crop losses, and enhance agricultural productivity through accessible digital tools.
